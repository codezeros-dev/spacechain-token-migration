What is SPC token?

SpaceChain(SPC) is a decentralized space agency with an open network driven by collaboration and transparency through blockchain technology implementation.

How it works

01. Connect Wallet

Connect your wallet with the application, through metamask or scanning a QR code through any ethereum wallets(https://walletconnect.org/wallets/)

02. Send SPC to Vault

Click on Send SPC to Vault button and approve the transaction from relevant wallet.

03.Upgrade SPC v1 tokens

Upgrade your token from V1 to V2, you can also verify the balance here.
